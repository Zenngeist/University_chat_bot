General Examination Instructions
Students should enter the examination hall 15 minutes before the scheduled time.

Students who arrive late will not be permitted to enter the examination hall.

Students must write their Roll Number on the question paper before starting the exam.

Students must occupy the seats assigned to them as per the seating plan.

All students are required to carry their University Identity Card.

Entry to the exam hall is not permitted without the University I-card and the examination schedule from the ERP portal.

All instructions on the answer booklet must be read carefully, and the cover page entries must be completed.

Students should not carry any material that could be considered for unfair means.

The flying squad is authorized to conduct physical checks during the examination.

Do not write your name or any identification mark in the answer booklet.

Students are not allowed to leave the examination hall without the permission of the invigilator.

Writing on desks or walls is considered malpractice.

Carrying programmable calculators, electronic gadgets, mobile phones, or books into the examination hall is considered malpractice.

Any rough work must be done on the last page of the answer booklet.
